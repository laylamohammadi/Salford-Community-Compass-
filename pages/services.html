Local Community Services

Services Available

  • Food Bank: Community Food Bank is open every Monday and Friday from 10 AM to 2 PM.
  • Health Clinic: Local Health Clinic offers free consultations on Wednesdays.
  • Library: The local library offers free resources and community programs.
  • Youth Center: Youth Center provides activities and support for local youth every weekend.
  • Senior Services: Programs available for seniors including meal delivery and social gatherings.

For more information, contact us at info@community.com
